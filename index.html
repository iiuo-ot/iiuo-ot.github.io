About IIUO.org

IIUO.org is a non-profit organization maintained by It Is Unique Official. We offer an open-source platform for registering and managing businesses, products, and subdomains — supporting entrepreneurs and creators to thrive in a connected world.

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities through accessible technology and open collaboration.

Our Focus Areas

Business Registration

Streamlined open-source tools to register and manage your business efficiently.

Product Management

Maintain and monitor product portfolios with simplicity and accessibility.

Subdomain Creation

Claim your digital presence securely under IIUO.org and grow globally.
